A HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) air purifier uses advanced filtration technology to remove 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 microns from the air, including dust, pollen, pet dander, and other allergens.

Our HEPA filters go through rigorous testing to meet the highest industry standards. They're constructed with a dense web of fibers that create a maze for airborne particles to navigate. As air flows through the filter, particles are captured through a combination of interception, impaction, and diffusion.

Unlike standard filters, true HEPA filters must meet specific efficiency standards. We use medical-grade HEPA filters that are 50% more effective than standard HEPA filters, capturing particles as small as 0.1 microns with 99.99% efficiency.
